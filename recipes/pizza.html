BBQ Pizza

Description

In this recipe we are creating a yummy BBQ chicken pizza to die for! These are great to make when having guests over for a party in the backyard or having a fun night in with your signifacnt other. Careful when making the pizza as it will leave you wanting to make more.

ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Red Onions
  • 6 oz of Chicken
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Lucilles BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Masage Dough on a flat surface
  2. Spread Marinara Sauce evenly across dought living half an inch of space on the outside for the crust
  3. Add Lucilles BBQ Sauce across the pizza
  4. With a spoon make sure to mix in the marinara sauce with the BBQ sauce so every bite has the taste of both
  5. Add Mozarella chesse on top of the Marinara Sauce
  6. Cut unions into small bite size postions to be spread across the pizza
  7. Place onions evenly across pizza
  8. Place chicken over pizza making sure every slice will have the same amount of chicken.
  9. Place in oven for 25 minutes at 350 degrees
  10. Cut pizza into slices and enjoy!
